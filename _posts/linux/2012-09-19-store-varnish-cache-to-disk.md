#### 方法一：在启动的时候设置
   
    varnishd -s file,/var/lib/varnish/varnish_storage.bin,50%  
 
#### 方法二：修改默认启动文件
 
   
    vi /etc/default/varnish  
  
    AEMON_OPTS="-a :9350 \  
                -T localhost:9351 \  
                -f /etc/varnish/default.vcl \  
                -S /etc/varnish/secret \  
                -s file,/mnt/varnish/varnish_storage.bin,200G"
